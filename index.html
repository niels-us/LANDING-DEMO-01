About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a study and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing, height, placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve you posture and make you more confortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the at hand


Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
